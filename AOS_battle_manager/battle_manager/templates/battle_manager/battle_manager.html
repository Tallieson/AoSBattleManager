What is This?

AOS Battle Manager is a web application borne of my desire to not carry 4 books to every game of Age of Sigmar. It allows you create battles and displays all relavent information on a single page. To goto a battle already created, enter it's battle ID in the search bar at the top.
If you have any suggestions or find any bugs, you can reach me at admin@aosbattlemanger.com. I hope you enjoy, and don't play Ironjawz!
